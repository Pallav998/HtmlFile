Maggie Ingredients

  1. Maggie One packet
  2. One cup water
  3. Salt according to your taste
  4. One pinch chilli powder

Maggie Recipe

    Take a pan
    Put water and Maggie in that pan
    Boil it for 2 minutes
    Add Maggie Masala , salt and chilli powder
    Boil it for 4 minutes
    Your Maggie is ready
