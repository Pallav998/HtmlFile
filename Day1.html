Steam MOMOS Recipe

Ingredients

  • White Flour - Maida
  • Cabbage
  • Carrot
  • Capsicum
  • Tomato
  • Red Chilli
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Pinch of Chilly Powder

For the filling

  • Chop shortly all the vegies
  • Make dow of White flour and put a little oil on it and cover it by a plate
  • Mix all vegies and add salt and chilly powder
  1. Making ball of white Flour and vegies
  2. In Momo pan boil water a little
  3. Put the momos on the streamer
  4. Cook it for 10 minutes
  5. Now you can Serve Momos with Chatni
