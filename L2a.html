Sushma Swaraj

1952 - 2019

The first female Spokesperson of a national political party in India.

Sushma Swaraj was born on 14 February 1952 at Ambala Cantonment, Haryana into a Punjabi Hindu Brahmin family, to Hardev Sharma and Shrimati Laxmi Devi. Her father was a prominent Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh member. Her parents hailed from the Dharampura area of Lahore , Pakistan . She was educated at Sanatan Dharma College in Ambala Cantonment and earned a bachelor's degree with majors in Sanskrit and Political Science.She studied law at Panjab University, Chandigarh.A state-level competition by the Language Department of Haryana saw her winning the best Hindi Speaker award for three consecutive years.Sushma Swaraj was a strict vegetarian. She served as the Minister of External Affairs of India from 2014 to 2019. Swaraj was known for her active use of social media to assist Indian citizens abroad. She played a significant role in shaping India's foreign policy and was admired for her compassion and dedication to public service. She passed away in 2019.
